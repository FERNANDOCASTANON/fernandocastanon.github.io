About Me

I am currently pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science. I am a highly motivated individual and self-taught Web Developer based in Los Angeles, California. My goal is to always provide amazing expiriences and quality websites to help bring anyone's ideas to the real world.

My introduction to programming was in my first programming course in colloge. At the time I was an Electrical Engineering major who knew nothing about programming. I just knew I wanted to build robots. I enrolled in a Java and C course because it was required for my major. While in that class I met real Hackers and Software Developers that already had jobs working in the industry and were taking the class "just for fun". I was amazed at these individuals, as well as my other classmates, how much they could do, what they knew. After taking those classes I decided to switch my major to Computer Science realizing that I can program robots as well.
