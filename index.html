I just knew I wanted to build robots. I enrolled in a Java and C course because it was required for my major. While in that class I met real Hackers and Software Developers that already had jobs working in the industry and were taking the class "just for fun". I was amazed at these individuals, as well as my other classmates, how much they could do, what they knew. After taking those classes I decided to switch my major to Computer Science.
